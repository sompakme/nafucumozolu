Jason Momoa And Lisa Bonet Move Back In Together After Divorce News

July 03, 2024
blog

Lisa Bonet and Jason Momoa are rumored to be giving their long-standing romance another shot, one month after they announced their divorce.

The actors first shared the split news in a joint statement, published on Momoa's Instagram account. One month in, a source told 'HollywoodLife' the pair are giving their love story another chance and have moved back in together.

Lisa Bonet And Jason Momoa Are Giving Their Marriage Another Shot

"Jason moved back in with Lisa about two weeks ago and they are very much back together. They decided to work on things rather than throw in the towel because they have so much invested in each other," the source alleged.

"Everyone is really hoping that they can make this work this time around," they continued, saying that the pair's two children, 14-year-old Lola Iolani and 13-year-old Nakoa-Wolf Manakauapo Namakaeha, are "obviously thrilled".

Bonet and Momoa first announced their divorce in January this year.

"We have all felt the squeeze and changes of these transformational times. A revolution is unfolding ~ and our family is of no exception . . . feeling and growing from the seismic shifts occurring. And so~ We share our Family news ~ That we are parting ways in marriage. We share this not because we think it's newsworthy ~ But so that~ as we go about our lives we may do so with dignity and honesty," the since-deleted note on the 'Game of Thrones' star's Instagram read.

"We free each other ~to be who we are learning to become…Our devotion unwavering to this sacred life & our Children […] Teaching our Children What’s possible," it also said.

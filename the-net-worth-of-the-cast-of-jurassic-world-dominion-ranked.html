The epic dino-franchise is set to return in the theatres on June 10 with Jurassic World Dominion. The film first began with Steven Spielberg's Jurassic Park in 1993 and now back in cinemas with their sixth installment. The film will be a mixture of old and new faces which excites the fans of the movie franchise. This will be American actor Chris Pratt’s second film on the franchise, four years after he starred in the 2018 film Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom. Take a look at the cast members and their net worth.

Isabella Sermon

Isabella Sermon will play as Maisie Lockwood in the film. Maisie Lockwood is newly adopted by Claire and Owen. She is the granddaughter of the former business partner of John Hammond, Sir Benjamin Lockwood. British actress Isabella Sermon has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million. The child actress born on July 8, 2006 became a star after she landed the role of Maisie Lockwood in the blockbuster film Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom.

Omar Sy

Omar Sy will play as Barry Sembène in the film. He is the raptor training assistant of Chris Pratt’s character Owen. He helps out in rampaging the Indominus Rex however was attacked by the lead Raptor Blue but he was saved thanks to Owen calling the dinosaurs off. He was later evacuated with the rest of the tourists in the place. Talented French on-screen actor and comedian Omar Sy has an estimated net worth of $5 million. He has starred in numerous prominent films including Transformers: The Last Knight and X-Men: Days of Future Past.

BD Wong

BD Wong will play as Henry Wu in the sixth installment of the franchise. He is the first character to cross over to the latest installment from the original 90s franchise. He is a true dinosaur OG and is a major character in the original novel where the movie is based from. Wong has stopped appearing in the franchise after Jurassic Park in 1993 however his character is still deemed important to the story. BD Wong has a massive net worth of $8 million thanks to his numerous TV, stage and film works. He is known for his portrayal in M. Butterfly which won him a Tony Award.

Sam Neill

Sam Neill will be playing as Alan Grant in the sixth installment of the movie franchise. Alan Grant is a world-renowned palaeontologist in the film. He is among the original cast where he arrived at the Isla Nublar way back with his partner Dr. Sattler in 1993's Jurassic Park. He has a lofty prehistory ambition which quickly turned to some disaster after he fought off with the T-Rex tyrants. Northern-Irish-born and New Zealander actor Sam Neill has an estimated net worth of $18 million. He is best known for his portrayal of the lead role in the original and first installments of the franchise Jurassic Park. He has since starred in numerous films and is now back to reprise his role.

Laura Dern

Laura Dern will be reprising her role as Ellie Sattler from the original Jurassic Park series. She is the first of the widely known trio from the first installments of the film to return to the franchise. Dr. Sattler is the feisty and adventurous paleobotanist who frequently saves her colleagues. She has a deep love for dinos and has a maternal care for them and often ignores the concerns of her male counterparts. Actress and producer Laura Dern has impressively amassed a net worth of $20 million. She rose to prominence after her performances in movies such as Blue Velvet, Mask, and Jurassic Park.

Bryce Dallas Howard

Bryce Dallas Howard will reprise her role as the park operations manager Claire Dearing in the film. Bryce Dallas Howard's character is often at the height of attacks from the dino world. She is a self proclaimed workaholic who will still go to work even during the attacks from the prehistoric monsters. Actress and director Bryce Dallas Howard has a massive net worth of $25 million. She is the eldest daughter of the famous filmmaker Ron Howard.

Jeff Goldblum

Jeff Goldblum plays as Ian MalcolmWay in the film. Jeff Goldblum is also part of the original 90s Jurassic Park cast members playing as the mathematician Ian Malcolm. The fans will once again see his chilled out charm and jaunty outfits. He is known in the story as the one who theorized the Chaos Theory about the dinosaurs which is a study of their unpredictable manners and behaviours. American actor Jeff Goldblum has an impressive net worth of $40 million. He has starred in numerous blockbuster films. He is also frequently seen portraying the unconventional characters in some indie films.

Chris Pratt

Chris Pratt will reprise his role as Owen Grady in the film Jurassic World Dominion. The Guardians of The Galaxy actor is back to spearhead his third escape from the prehistoric monsters. He will be returning to the franchise as the Velociraptor trainer who helps out in fending off some unwanted attacks all while winning the heart of Claire. American actor Chris Pratt has a massive net worth of $80 million. The actor rose to prominence with his role on the NBC series Parks and Recreation playing as Andy Dwyer.

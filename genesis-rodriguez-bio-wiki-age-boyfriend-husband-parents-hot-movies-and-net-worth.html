Genesis Rodriguez Career

She continued private instruction and obtained a recurring role on the American daytime series Days of Our Lives from November 2005 through January 2006. She as well repeats special guest on the Bravo TV series Top Chef. In addition to American television, Rodriguez explored Spanish language roles and played as two dual leads as Rosita Amado and Violeta Hurtado in the primetime series Dame Chocolate, which aired on Telemundo.

She had a substantial role as the love interest of Will Ferrell in the movie comedy Casa de Mi Padre in 2012, a spoof in the style of the Mexican soap operas of the 1970s. She then followed that role with an appearance in The Last Stand in 2013. She played villainous FBI Agent Ellen Richards, She appeared in the comedy Identity Thief as Marisol. Rodriguez lent her voice to Honey Lemon in the 2014 film Big Hero 6.

In February 2017 she appeared, in the main female role, in the music video for Romeo Santos’ song Héroe Favorito.

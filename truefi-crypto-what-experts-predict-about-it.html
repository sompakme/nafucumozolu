TrueFi Crypto: What Experts Predict About It

September 27, 2024
blog

TrueFi is an uncollateralized lending DeFi protocol, powered by the TRU token and an on-chain credit model — the first of its type.

TrueFi loans are made to vetted, institutional-grade borrowers like Nibbio, Alameda Research, Amber Group, and Wintermute Trading, who, once approved, get access to fast cryptocurrency loans with no collateral requirements.

Decisions on new borrowers joining the platform, the terms of each loan, and the overall direction of the TrueFi protocol are in the hands of TRU holders, who conduct such activities in return for token rewards in the form of TRU and a cut of the interested generated by the platform’s lending activity.

TrueFi was made possible by a union of centralized and decentralized finance. Launched by TrustToken, known for launching and operating the popular TUSD stablecoin, TrueFi is moving quickly through progressive decentralization.

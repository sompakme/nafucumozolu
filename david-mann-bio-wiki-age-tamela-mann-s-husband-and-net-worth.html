David Mann Biography and Wiki

David Mann (born David Anthony Mann Sr.) is an American gospel singer, stand up comedian, producer, and actor who has starred in many Tyler Perry play, including I, Can Do Bad All By Myself, Madea’s Family Reunion, Madea’s Class Reunion, Meet the Browns, and What’s Done in the Dark.

His character Mr. Brown is the father of Madea’s daughter Cora (wife – Tamela Mann). Mann also reprised his role as the character in the movies Tyler Perry’s Meet the Browns and Tyler Perry’s Madea Goes to Jail.

After This Video From Sumner Stroh, Fans Are Second-Guessing The Adam Levine Accusations

Last summer, Influencer and Instagram Model Sumner Stroh came forward with allegations that Maroon 5 lead vocalist Adam Levine had been cheating on his Victoria's Secret model wife Behati Prinsloo. Stroh took to TikTok to reveal Levine's infidelity saying "I was having an affair with a man who's married to a Victoria's Secret model. At the time, I was young, I was naive, and, I mean, quite frankly, I feel exploited. I wasn’t in ‘the scene’ like I am now, so I was definitely very easily manipulated. My morals were unknowingly compromised. I was completely manipulated."

In a series of screenshots revealed by Sumner throughout the video, it was alleged that Adam Levine wanted to name his third child after her. The "supposed" text from Adam read, "OK serious question. I’m having another baby, and if it’s a boy I really wanna name it Sumner. You OK with that? DEAD serious." Netizens were quick to criticize Adam but Stroh was not immune from backlash either. Users made parodies with her "new to LA" excuse. Others were quick to point out that Stroh stating that he was a Victoria's Secret model's husband instead of addressing him as the "Maroon 5 singer" in itself was the biggest clue that she was chasing fame.

Did Sumner Stroh Make Up The Affair For Fame?

A Reddit user posted this video from Sumner Stroh from a long back in which Stroh looked utterly different. In the TikTok video, Sumner gushes about singer and rapper Lil Nas X for being "a genius" for making a controversial music video for Call Me By Your Name.

"He knew what he was doing, he knew he was making a controversial video and that people would talk about it as a result and then of course blow it up because everyone would go to watch it and talk about it & literally argue over it," said Sumner in this video from early 2021.

Why Did Sumner Stroh Release The Video In September 2022?

If there is something that immediately made a lot of netizens suspicious it was the timing of the reveal of the supposed affair by Sumner Stroh. There were lots of speculation from fans that Stroh was secretly jealous of Behati Prinsloo, Adam's model wife.

A commenter on TikTok pointed out that Stroh had used the phrase "man who's married to a Victoria's Secret model" to describe Adam Levine, a man who is famous in his own right.

In the original video made by Sumner Stroh, she claimed that her friend tried to sell the story to a tabloid. But Morgan Stewart and Hunter March who are both hosts on E's Nightly Pop allege that they got DMs from Sumner revealing both the affair and Behati's pregnancy to them.

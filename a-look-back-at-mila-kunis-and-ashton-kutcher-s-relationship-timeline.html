A Look Back At Mila Kunis And Ashton Kutchers Relationship Timeline

August 18, 2024
blog

Hollywood produces countless romantic movies each year. However, the most incredible Hollywood love stories happen offscreen. It's common for many famous couples to seem perfect on the surface, but be trainwrecks in reality. Fans all over the world hope that Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher's fantastic love story ends as beautifully as it began, rather than derailing.

Mila and Ashton are one of the most adorable couples in Hollywood. Many fans believe that they may be the one Hollywood couple that makes it. They have a long history that dates back to when they were teenagers. Indeed, they have a strong friendship that developed years before they were an item.

Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher's love story spans several years. They both lived a lot before they finally ended up together. They were both in other relationships that seemed destined to last. They were both in high-profile relationships, which gained a lot of media attention.

The timeline of their relationship offers insight into their love story. They went from being co-stars and friends to a married couple with kids. The timeline tells a tale of two soul mates meant for each other.

It's time to take A Look Back At Mila Kunis And Ashton Kutcher's Relationship Timeline.

20 1998 – Ashton Kutcher And Mila Kunis Play Love Interests On That '70s Show

In 1998, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher first met as teenagers, while working on the hit Fox sitcom, That 70's Show. They portrayed love interests, Jackie Burkhart and Michael Kelso. Jackie and Kelso had an intense love that went up and down. They appeared to be soul mates, but they didn't end up together.

At the end of the series, Kelso moves away to Chicago and Jackie ends up with Fez. Of course, Jackie and Kelso might have gotten back together in the 80s. However, at the time, Kunis and Kutcher were only friends offscreen.

19 1999 – They Share Their First Onscreen Kiss

Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis shared their first kiss in 1999. It was an onscreen kiss that happened when Kelso and Jackie took their relationship to the next level. However, Mila and Ashton had a different relationship offscreen. They developed a close platonic friendship and were almost like brother and sister.

Mila has lied about her age and was only fourteen at the time of the kiss. It was also Mila's first kiss in her whole life.

Kutcher said, "It was awkward because I'm like a 19-year-old kid. She was 14! She was like my little sister. I wanted to make sure she was okay."

18 2000 - Ashton Helps Mila With Her Chemistry Homework

As noted, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher developed a friendship during their time on That '70s Show. They were strictly friends and nothing more. Of course, Mila was still in high school during the first season. Kutcher often helped Kunis with her homework...in particular, her chemistry homework.

Kutcher noted, "Yeah, I did her chemistry homework for her. I think I was her first kiss, like, on the show. We have our first kiss memorialized on a TV show!"

17 2002 - Mila Starts Dating Macaulay Culkin 

Despite being love interests on TV, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher had zero attraction to each other. By this point, That '70s Show was a significant hit, and the teenage cast were all notable stars.

In 2002, Kunis began dating the most famous child star in Hollywood history, Macaulay Culkin. Culkin starred in the Christmas classic, Home Alone, when he was a kid. Fans often mobbed Kunis and Culkin, trying to get close to Culkin.

16 2005 – Ashton Leaves That '70s Show

The teenage cast of That '70s Show all became world-famous within a few years. However, Ashton Kutcher was the breakout star. He stood out among the cast and his career took off.

Soon enough, he began focusing on his film career and completed filming of The Butterfly Effect. In 2005, Kutcher decided it was time to leave the show. Of course, the series tried to continue without Kutcher and Topher Grace (Eric Foreman). Kutcher returned for a few episodes in the final season, as well as the series finale.

15 2005 – Mila Gets Punk’D

Ashton Kutcher left That '70s Show and embarked on a movie career and more. He famously created and hosted the MTV series, Punk'd. The series featured Kutcher pulling pranks on his celebrity friends.

In 2005, Kunis appeared in an episode...unbeknownst to her. During the episode, Kunis helps a woman who is speaking Russian to save her dog. Of course, everything that could go wrong does. Kunis is about to flip out when Kutcher reveals it's a prank.

14 September 2005 – Ashton Marries Demi Moore

In 2005, Ashton Kutcher made major headlines all over the world because he started dating Demi Moore. In the 90s, Moore was one of the highest-paid actresses in Hollywood. Kutcher and Moore were a high-profile couple who attracted a huge amount of media attention.

In September of 2005, Kutcher and Moore went a step further and married. They remained a subject of media fascination for several years.

13 2011 – Mila And Macaulay Break Up

Mila Kunis and Macaulay Culkin were a private couple. They stayed out of the spotlight. Indeed, they even broke up in a low-key manner. In 2011, Kunis and Culkin split up and went their separate ways. They had dated for roughly eight years, throughout most of their 20s.

At the time, Kunis' rep put out a statement calling it an amicable split. However, Kunis now admits that it was a horrible breakup, which she didn't do a proper job of handling.

12 November 2011 – Ashton And Demi Announce Divorce

2011 was a big year for Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis, although they might not have realized it. Kutcher and Demi Moore made major headlines when they announced their intention to divorce. The news sent shockwaves around the world...most fans couldn't believe it. However, Moore and Kutcher were having problems behind the scenes that most fans were unaware of at the time.

11 January 2012 – Mila And Ashton Reconnect At The 69th Golden Globe Awards

In 2012, Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis reconnected at the Golden Globe Awards. They were both newly single and ready to mingle. They didn't get romantic at the start of the night. By the end of the night, however, Kunis had a change of heart.

She noted, "I was like looking around, and there was like a really beautiful man from the back. I was like, 'What a very nice stature of a gentleman.' I was literally, like, 'Ooh, he's kind of hot.' I was single. And then he turned around, and I was like, 'Oh, my god! It's Kutch!'"

10 February 2012 - They Share Their First Offscreen Kiss At Ashton's House Warming Party And Start A Casual Relationship

After reconnecting at the Golden Globe Awards, Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis spent more time together. Kutcher actually tried to set Kunis up on a blind date with his friend, at his house warming party. However, things went a different way that night.

That night, Kunis and Kutcher shared their first offscreen kiss. Unlike the first kiss, there was a lot of passion. They soon started a casual relationship that went on for a few weeks. Ironically, they both starred in movies, Friends With Benefits and No Strings Attached, which had plots that were similar to their real-life love story.

9 April 2012 - They Commit To Being In An Exclusive Relationship And Move In Together 

After a few weeks, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher's relationship changed drastically. Kunis and Kutcher were becoming more serious. Kunis finally revealed her true feelings. Kunis wanted a commitment or she'd walk away. He showed up the next day and they began dating each other exclusively. Indeed, he even asked her to move in with him. They moved in together a few weeks later.

8 February 2013 – Giving Each Other Love Eyes At LA Lakers Game

Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher started making headlines. They attempted to keep the relationship a secret, but that was impossible. Kutcher and Kunis soon started being a little more public. They were often spotted supporting various Los Angeles sports teams, such as the Dodgers, Clippers, and Lakers.

In 2013, Kutcher and Kunis attended a Lakers game but paid more attention to each other. They couldn't stop looking at each other (and giving each other love eyes) the entire night.

7 November 2013 – Ashton And Demi Officially Divorce

As noted, Ashton Kutcher and Demi Moore announced their intention to divorce in 2011. They had separated two years earlier and Kutcher already moved on. Regardless, neither one of them filled for divorce, which dragged things out.

Kutcher didn't want to waste any more time and filed for divorce to get the ball rolling. In 2013, one of the most famous couples in Hollywood history called it quits. Kutcher and Moore's relationship came to a sad ending. However, that opened the door for Kutcher to make more of a commitment to Mila Kunis.

6 February 2014 – Ashton And Mila Get Engaged

Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher were both free to do whatever they wanted to. Kunis and Kutcher live a very private life for the most part. Indeed, they have opened up to the public on only a few occasions.

In 2014, Kutcher and Kunis became engaged. At the same time, Kunis became aware that she was pregnant. The child ended up being born before Kunis and Kutcher could walk down the aisle. Regardless, Kunis made the world jealous when she subtly showed off her ring.

5 September 30, 2014 – Mila Gives Birth To Daughter Wyatt

Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher didn't wait until after their wedding day to start their family. Of course, they usually do things their way and don't care what people think. After getting engaged, Kunis revealed to the world that she was pregnant. That meant they had to push the wedding day back a bit.

In September of 2014, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher welcomed their daughter, Wyatt, into the world. However, they were about to add to their family.

4 July 4, 2015 – Ashton And Mila Get Married

Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher do their best to be a private couple. It's tough for them since everyone's always trying to take their picture. Their relationship moved fast, but that's because of the friendship they had developed as teenagers.

On July 4, 2015, Kunis and Kutcher married in a private ceremony. They invited their closest friends and family members. Also, they didn't want to spend a lot of money on the event. Kunis purchased the wedding bands from Etsy.

3 November 30, 2016 – Mila Gives Birth To Son Dimitri

On That '70s Show, Jackie Burkhart wanted to marry Michael Kelso. Of course, Kelso freaked out and ran off to California. In real life, Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis wasted no time at all. They got engaged, started a family, and then married.

Of course, the Kutcher-Kunis family keeps growing. On November 30, 2016, Kunis and Kutcher welcomed their son, Dimitri, to the world. They're the perfect little family that all Hollywood families strive to be.

2 September 2019 – Demi's Controversial Tell-All Book Portrays Ashton In A Negative Light And Upsets Mila's Mother

In September of 2019, Demi Moore released a tell-all book, Inside Out, that painted Ashton Kutcher in a negative light. Moore notes she went through severe depression while married to Kutcher. She confirmed rumors that Kutcher had been unfaithful several times. According to Moore, he also pressured her to 'experiment'.

The book created a great deal of controversy and upset Mila Kunis's mother. She was very upset about the claims of infidelity.

1 November 2019 – Ashton And Mila Ignore Controversy And Go On A Date To Disneyland

Despite the controversy, Mila Kunis stood by her husband, Ashton Kutcher. Demi Moore's book tour only drew more attention to the controversy. However, Kunis and Kutcher put that behind them and went on a date.

In November 2019, Kunis, Kutcher, and the kids headed to Disneyland for the day. They had a fun day at the park with their kids, Wyatt and Dimitri. The couple was spotted having a good time.

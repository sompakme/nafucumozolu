Steph Curry, the renowned basketball player for the Golden State Warriors, has multiple residences in California. Let’s take a closer look at his luxurious homes in Orinda, Alamo, and Atherton.

In 2013, Curry purchased a stunning 4,900-square-foot home in Orinda for $3.1 million. This property served as his primary residence until he decided to upgrade in 2015. He then acquired a larger home in Walnut Creek, and after two years, the Curry family made Alamo their main base. Their Alamo residence is a magnificent 10,290-square-foot mansion that offers both comfort and elegance. They later added a second home in Alamo in 2018, further solidifying their connection to the community.

However, Curry’s quest for the perfect California dream home didn’t stop there. In 2020, he set his sights on Atherton, known for its exclusive neighborhoods and multi-million dollar properties. Curry purchased a sprawling 17,761-square-foot mansion in Atherton for a staggering $30 million. This palatial estate showcases his success and reflects the glamorous lifestyle enjoyed by many A-list celebrities.

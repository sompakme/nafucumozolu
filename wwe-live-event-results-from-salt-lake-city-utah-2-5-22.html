WWE Live Event Results From Salt Lake City, Utah (2/5/22)

September 09, 2024
blog

WWE held a ‘Road to WrestleMania’ live event in Salt Lake City over the weekend where numerous superstars showcased their in-ring skills. Check out the results below.

  • Kevin Owens defeated The Miz
  • Riddle defeated Chad Gable
  • Omos defeated Montez Ford
  • WWE SmackDown Women’s Championship: Charlotte Flair defeated Naomi with Sonya Deville as special guest referee
  • Ricochet defeated Austin Theory
  • WWE RAW Women’s Championship: Becky Lynch defeated Liv Morgan, Doudrop, and Bianca Belair
  • Big E, Kofi Kingston, and Seth Rollins defeated Roman Reigns & The Usos

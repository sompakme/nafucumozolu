The Truth About Pete Davidson And Adam Sandler's Relationship

September 28, 2024
blog

At first glance, it may not seem like Saturday Night Live (SNL) cast member Pete Davison and comedian Adam Sandler would know each other quite well. However, what many may not realize is that they have much in common. In fact, Sandler was once an SNL cast member himself before he wrote and starred in his first-ever film.

Since then, Sandler has returned to host SNL and performed some sketches with Davidson. At the same time, the two actors have also been hanging out behind the scenes.

Pete Nearly Worked With Adam When He Was A Younger

Back in the 90s, Sandler co-wrote and starred in the comedy-drama Big Daddy. In the film, he plays a law school grad who decides to adopt a kid to impress his girlfriend. The role of the kid eventually went to twin brothers Cole and Dylan Sprouse. What many don’t realize, however, is that Davidson also auditioned for the part.

“In 1999, my mom took me to audition for Big Daddy,” the SNL cast member revealed on Instagram as he captioned a behind-the-scenes photo from the time that Sandler hosted SNL. “20 years later I finally got it.”

Pete Looks Up To Adam

Ever since they became friends, Davidson has nothing but praise for the veteran comedian. “He’s definitely the best,” Davidson said of Sandler during a guest appearance on Late Night with Seth Meyers. “Just like caring and sweet and not full of crap. Which is very rare to find.”

At the same time, Davidson also pointed out that he’s been trying to emulate Sandler. “Also, the way he carries himself I try to follow,” Davidson explained during a roundtable discussion for The Hollywood Reporter. “He’s so kind to everyone, and you never hear of a Sandler issue — there’s never like a Sandler-gate. Any time you see that guy’s face, it’s associated with smiles and good vibes. That’s the thing I’m trying to follow.”

Not only that, Davidson also sees Sandler as his personal style icon. “I pretty much just took whatever Adam Sandler was wearing and have tried to do that, because he's my favorite fashion icon,” Davidson explained during an interview with Popsugar. “If you look at any photo of Sandler on a set, or when he's in his regular clothes, he's just the coolest, most confident and comfortable person you'll ever see. I've tried to adapt that type of style now.”

Today, Davidson and Sandler are also as close as can be. “I’m pretty tight with the Sandman, which is the coolest thing I ever get to say,” Davidson said. “He’s just such a cool, nice guy. I can’t explain how wonderful of a person he is.”

They Teamed Up While Everyone Was In Quarantine

As it turns out, not even a pandemic can stop Davidson and Sandler from doing something fun together. In fact, they teamed up for the Quarantine Song. Davidson came up with the idea and immediately checked if his comedian friend would be willing to sing along too. “I just reached out and texted him and was like, ‘Hey, is there any shot you would ever want to do a song together?’” Davidson recalled. “And he was like, ‘Hell yeah, buddy’ and it was awesome.”

Soon, the two men worked on the song virtually. “We just sent each other notes back and forth and he came up with that great verse,” Davidson revealed. “It just fell together. I was really thrilled.” The video also features Sandler hanging around his home with underpants on his face. At some point, the actor sings, “I’m so bored I’m doin’ songs with Pete.”

Pete Also Consulted With Adam On A Possible Career Move

Especially in recent weeks, there has been talk of Davidson making his exit from SNL. As it turns out, the actor has been contemplating on making a departure lately. In fact, Davidson recently remarked, “I’m surprised I made it to seven. I’m ready to hang up the jersey.” The actor also lamented that when it comes to materials on the show, “so few come my way that I have to do them all.”

Interestingly, Davidson also revealed that he’s even consulted with Sandler about leaving the long-running sketch comedy show. The advice that Davidson got was that he has to time things perfectly. “You don’t ever want to pull the trigger too early,” he explained while speaking with Charlamagne Tha God on a YouTube show. “Everybody’s always been like, ‘You’ll know when you know, and it’ll be all right.'”

At the moment, Sandler and Davidson are yet to do a film (or series together). That said, it is worth noting that both men have ongoing projects with Netflix with Davidson set to star in the film I Slept with Joey Ramone and Sandler’s Netflix deal extended in 2020. Hence, it may be possible for these actors to finally do a film together.

After all, Davidson has always been a fan of the Sandler-verse. “I like what Sandler did where he’s like, ‘These are my eight friends, we’re going to do this formula for the next 30 to 50 years.’ He built this entire universe for himself, and he’s in his own lane.” Perhaps, Davidson will become a part of it soon enough.

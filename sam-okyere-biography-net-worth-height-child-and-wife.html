Sam Okyere is a popular Ghanaian television personality active in South Korea and Ghana and best known as a political commentator.

Sam Okyere was born Samuel Nana Kwesi Tweneboah Koduah Okyere Gyan Darko on April 21, 1991, in Accra, Ghana. He posts his parents and siblings on social media but has not disclosed their names.

Sam Okyere matriculated from a local school in Ghana before he was accepted into the Korean Government Scholarship Program in 2009. He moved to Korea to study computer science and engineering at Sogang University, in Seoul.

Sam Okyere made a debut on television in 2013 when he appeared on the tvN series Island Village Teacher. He became more known and controversial when he condemned the use of Blackface in a graduation photo by a student from a high school in South Korea on Instagram in 2020.

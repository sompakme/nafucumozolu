6 What Did Kris Jenner's Former Assistants Say About Her Work Ethics?

Kris Jenner's former assistant, Pam Behan, said she tended to flip out when things didn't go her way.

The ever-efficient momager has several personal and executive assistants who work with her to run the day-to-day affairs of her family's business empire. Over the years, Kris Jenner has proven to be reality TV gold and a business mogul who knows how to stay trending. It is safe to say that thanks to her selection of assistants, she has been able to successfully execute goals to keep the family business afloat.

Like every boss, the TV star has a set of rules she expects her staff to follow by the book, and if an assistant is found wanting, they will definitely face the consequences. She once confirmed this to HuffPost in 2018, noting that a breach of privacy will not go unpunished, even if the person was not financially buoyant. Kris Jenner revealed:

"I don't care how much money somebody might have. Some people think, 'Oh, I don't have any money, and they are not going to sue me.' Well, we'll take payments. We'll garner those wages for, you know, the next 10 years..."

His fans argued that such behavior was highly inappropriate for someone in Haddon’s position as a pastor and a gospel singer.

The criticism towards Haddon and his wife’s dancing video prompted him to address the controversy and share his perspective on the matter.

He drew inspiration from the lyrics of the song “People Make the World Go Round” by the Stylistics.

Haddon aimed to promote understanding and acceptance of different viewpoints. 

However, despite Haddon’s efforts to foster unity, the reaction to his Instagram post remained divided.

In July 2013, Deitrick Haddon married Dominique Mctyer, and together they have three children: Destin, Denver, and the youngest, Deitrick.

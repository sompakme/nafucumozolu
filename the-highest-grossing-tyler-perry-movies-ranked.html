Tyler Perry is one of Hollywood's most successful filmmakers, having produced diverse films that have generated millions of dollars worldwide. Perry was born on September 13, 1969, in New Orleans, Louisiana, and grew up in poverty. He began his career as a screenwriter, writing and producing stage plays in the 1990s that dealt with family, faith, and personal redemption themes.

Perry's ability to continuously generate commercially successful films has propelled him to the forefront of the film business. He has also directed and produced several other successful films, solidifying his position as a principal Hollywood director. This list of Tyler Perry's highest-grossing films demonstrates his aptitude for crafting films that viewers adore and are prepared to pay to see on the big screen.

The Single Moms Club - $16 Million

The movie is about five single mothers from different places who get together to help each other and get through the hard parts of having kids on their own. Even though the movie didn't do as well as some of Perry's other films, it still has the same themes of family, community, and determination as many of his other works. Even though it made less money at the box office, The Single Moms Club is still a moving and inspiring movie many people enjoy.

Boo, 2! A Madea Halloween - $48 Million

Via Flickr/gharip

Madea and company battle otherworldly forces in a haunted campsite in the 2017 horror-comedy sequel. Boo, 2! A Madea Halloween was less successful than its predecessor, but its mix of humor and scares still attracted viewers. Despite conflicting reviews, the film's box office success proves Perry's ability to make films that appeal to a broad audience. Perry is a talented filmmaker with a distinct voice and passionate fans.

Why Did I Get Married? - $55 Million

Via Flickr/mystikangel07

Four couples are followed as they travel to a couples retreat to work on their relationships. Janet Jackson, Jill Scott, and Tyler Perry himself are among the ensemble cast members. The film Why Did I Get Married? explored issues including love, commitment, and betrayal while receiving mixed reviews from reviewers. Nevertheless, it was a commercial success because of Perry's following base. The sequel to the film Why Did I Get Married Too? was released in 2010 and joined the ranks of Tyler Perry's highest-grossing productions.

Madea's Witness Protection - $55 Million

Via Flickr/Kassia Blakeney

Madea's Witness Protection takes the fifth spot on the list of Tyler Perry's highest-grossing movies, earning $55 million worldwide. The 2012 film follows Madea, the beloved character played by Perry, as she takes in a family in witness protection. The movie also stars Eugene Levy, Denise Richards, and Doris Roberts. The film's box office success is a testament to Perry's ability to create compelling stories with characters that audiences love. While some critics felt that the movie relied too heavily on Madea's shtick, fans embraced the film's humor and heart. Madea's Witness Protection is another example of Perry's ability to create movies that appeal to a broad audience while remaining true to his unique style and voice.

Why Did I Get Married Too? - $60 Million

Via Flickr/theomnivore2

Why Did I Get Married Too? made $60 million worldwide and is Tyler Perry's fourth highest-grossing film. This 2010 comedy-drama sequel to Perry's 2007 film Why Did I Get Married? That grossed $55 million. Both films follow friends who travel together each year and examine marriage and relationships. Why Did I Get Married Too? stars Janet Jackson, Tyler Perry, Jill Scott, and Malik Yoba. Audiences loved the film's performances and balance of comedy and drama. These films made Perry a go-to director for relationship and family dramas.

Madea's Family Reunion - $63 Million

Via Flickr/devinicole

The film, which came out in 2006, depicts Madea assisting her niece Lisa with wedding preparations while coping with her problems. The film stars Tyler Perry, Blair Underwood, Lynn Whitfield, and Cicely Tyson. Although Madea's Family Reunion was met with mixed reviews from film critics, it proved to be a commercial success, proving that Perry had hit on something moviegoers couldn't get enough of. The film's loving tale and memorable characters have made it a critical and commercial success, and it stands out even among Perry's other works.

Madea Goes To Jail - $90 Million

Madea Goes to Jail ranks second on the list of Tyler Perry's highest-grossing films. The 2009 film tells the tale of Madea, who is sent to prison following a high-speed police chase. The film features an all-star cast, which includes Tyler Perry, Derek Luke, Keshia Knight Pulliam, and Viola Davis. The global total for Madea Goes to Jail was an impressive $90 million, solidifying Perry's position as a box office titan. The film's success also prepared the way for subsequent Madea films, which have become a staple of Perry's filmography.

Boo! A Madea Halloween - $103 Million

Via Flickr/Series Peliculas Coatza

Boo! A Madea Halloween,which grossed over $103 million worldwide, is Tyler Perry's highest-grossing film. In this 2016 comedy-horror film, Madea (played by the inimitable Tia Carrere) must deal with a gang of rowdy youngsters on Halloween night. Perry's skill at fusing comedy and horror resulted in a fresh, engaging film with a worldwide audience. The film's success at the box office proves Perry's ability to develop films that resonate with audiences and highlight his style as a director. Unsurprisingly, Boo! A Madea Halloween is one of Perry's most financially successful films.

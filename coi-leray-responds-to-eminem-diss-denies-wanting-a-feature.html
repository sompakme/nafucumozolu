Coi Leray Responds To Eminem Diss, Denies Wanting A Feature

August 30, 2024
blog

Coi Leray is unbothered by Eminem‘s latest diss record taking aim at her father, Benzino, despite the fact that he name-dropped her, as well.

On new track “Doomsday Pt. 2”, Eminem rhymes, “Well, I guess then I regret to inform you, hate to spoil the day (What?)/But this doesn’t bring me no joy to say (Huh?)/Guess that Coi Leray feat’s in the toilet, ayy? (Godd**n).”

While Em spit of flushing a verse for Leray down the pipes, the “Players” artist took to X/Twitter to distance herself from the beef, as well as any notion that she wanted a feature from the Detroit MC.

“Rap beef is so washed and tired. Exhausting. Embarrassing. Just f**king over all corny as f**k,” she began, adding, “I got no issues with no one. I’m so locked in on my grown and sexy vibes… if anybody don’t like me, that’s something they gotta take on with them selves,” followed by a heart emoji. She then addressed Em by name, captioning a sexy clip of herself, “Man who the hell said I wanted a Eminem feature ?? Imagine Eminem on ‘Wanna Come thru?'” Check it out below.

Eminem raps on the new song, “Now I got a riddle (What?), one condition, you mustn’t laugh (Okay)/What is the opposite of Benzino? (Uh, what?)/A giraffe (Haha), Go at his neck, how the f**k is that? (Yeah)/How can I go at somethin’ he doesn’t have? (Haha)/Arm so short he can’t even touch his hands/When they’re up above his head doin’ jumpin’ jacks (Wow).” 

Benzino has since shared his reaction to “Doomsday Pt. 2.”

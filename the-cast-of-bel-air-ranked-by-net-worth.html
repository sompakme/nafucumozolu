The Cast Of 'Bel-Air,' Ranked By Net Worth

July 22, 2024
blog

"She said, 'You're moving with your auntie and uncle in Bel-Air.''' The new Fresh Prince of Bel-Air reboot, Bel-Air, is ready to stream on Peacock on February 13. Executive-produced by Will Smith, the reboot features a whole new cast, with the same characters but re-imagined in modern-day America. The show is supposed to be more of a drama instead of a comedy, as Will is set to tackle second chances and biases.

With a new series comes a new cast. Some of them are newcomers to the acting world, while others are veterans to it. It would be impossible to rank the actors by skill having not seen the show yet, so the only way to do it would be from lowest to highest net worth. If this show is a success, these net worths are subject to change. Here is the cast of Bel-Air, ranked by net worth.

10 Jabari Banks - $100,000 To $125,000

Jabari Banks plays the main character of Will Smith, who gets shipped to Bel-Air, CA, from West Philadelphia, after he gets into some trouble. Despite him being the lead character of the show, his net worth is not that high. Many websites put his net worth at under $150,000, in the range of $100,000 to $125,000. Considering Bel-Air is his first acting job, it is not surprising that he doesn't have a high net worth. Banks probably only accumulated that much from working on the show and promo.

9 Olly Sholotan - $150,000

Olly Sholotan plays Carlton Banks, Will's cousin who get good grades and never gets into trouble. His net worth is estimated to be around $150,000. He is just starting out in his career, having some of his music featured in shorts and feature length projects. Sholotan also appeared in the 2020 Venice International Film Festival Selection, Run Hide Fight. He also just finished filming the movie, Evolution of Nate Gibson.

8 Jordan L. Jones - $100,000 To $1 Million

Jordan L. Jones is an actor known for Snowfall, The Rookie and Rel. He is going to play Jazz, Will's best friend, in the upcoming reboot. In the original Jazz was known for getting thrown out of the house by Uncle Phil. Jones has also starred in other TV series, music videos and short films. His net worth is estimated to be between $100,000 to $1 million. Websites are currently reporting different things, with no definite answer amongst them.

7 Coco Jones - $1 Million

Coco Jones will play the role of Hilary Banks, Will's older cousin who loves to spend her dad's money and hasn't gone to college. Jones, no relation to Jordan L. Jones, rose to prominence on the Disney Channel show, Let It Shine and starred in other shows on the network and off. Between her acting and singing career, Jones has amassed a net worth of $1 million.

6 Akira Akbar - $1 To $3 Million

Despite only being 15 years old, Akira Akbar's net worth is estimated to be between $1 to $3 million. In Bel-Air, Akbar will play Ashley Banks, Will's youngest cousin, who idolizes him. However, you may have seen her in some other roles before this. She is best known for her role as Monia Rambeau in Captain Marvel. Akbar has also starred in We Can Be Heroes, Criminal Minds, Grey's Anatomy and more.

5 Simone Joy Jones - $1 To $5 Million

Simone Joy Jones is an actress known for her roles in The Son of No One, What If?, The Chair and American Rust. She will play Lisa, Will's love interest, who was originally played by Nia Long. Simone Joy Jones' net worth is estimated to be between $1 to $5 million, according to multiple websites.

4 Tyler Barnhardt - $1 To $5 Million

Tyler Barnhardt is set to play a new character, Connor Satterfield, who is Carlton's best friend, lacrosse teammate and the second most popular kid in school, after Carlton. You may know Barnhardt from the series 13 Reasons Why. He has starred in other minor roles too and is set to play Young Blaine in the upcoming film, Senior Year. His net worth is estimated to be between $1 to $5 million.

3 Cassandra Freeman - $1.9 Million

Cassandra Freeman will play Vivian Banks, or Aunt Viv to Will. As of this year, her net worth is estimated to be around $1.9 million. You may know Freeman from Blue Bloods, Atlanta, The Last OG, or the other series and shorts she has acted in. Being older and having had more experience in the entertainment business, it is no wonder her net worth is higher.

2 Adrian Holmes - $4 Million

Adrian Holmes will play Phillip Banks, or Uncle Phil to Will. Holmes net worth is $4 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He is a Welsh-Canadian actor who has more than 130 acting credits to his name, including Arrow, 19-2 and Christmas movies. He has earned most of his money through acting and has even won a Canadian Screen Award.

1 Jimmy Akingbola - $4 Million

Jimmy Akingbola's net worth is estimated to also be $4 million. The Nigerian actor is best known for his roles in Arrow, The Crouches and Doctors, with a lot more acting credits to his name. Besides film and TV acting, Akingbola has also earned his net worth through theater acting, most notable in Thumbelina and Henry VIII.

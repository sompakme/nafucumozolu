1 Tom Holland And Zendaya Finally Admit They Are A Couple

Date: November 2021

  • Zendaya and Tom Holland finally talk about one another and their relationship and how in love they are.

While speculation had run rampant that there were always feelings between Zendaya and Tom, the two did not admit that they were in any type of relationship until they were caught kissing in a car via paparazzi in July 2021 while at a stop light.

After this picture was published by several media outlets, the duo is not quite as coy about talking about each other. Instead, in November 2021, both Tom and Zendaya admitted that while they love what they do, being famous does have its downsides, like not being able to keep what happens in their private lives private, such as a stolen kiss at a stoplight.

"One of the downsides of our fame is that privacy isn’t really in our control anymore, and a moment that you think is between two people that love each other very much is now a moment that is shared with the entire world," Tom said.

Zendaya went on to say, "The equal sentiment [we both share] is just that when you really love and care about somebody, some moments or things, you wish were your own.…I think loving someone is a sacred thing and a special thing and something that you want to deal with and go through and experience and enjoy amongst the two people that love each other."

Once the cat was out of the bag, however, there seemed to be a bit of weight lifted off of both Zendaya and Tom's shoulders. No longer did they have to keep their relationship a secret. But, if fans are looking for them to dish on any details of what they do when they are not on the red carpet or on set, they have another thing coming.

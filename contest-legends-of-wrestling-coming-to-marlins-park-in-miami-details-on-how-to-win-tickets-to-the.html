CONTEST: Legends Of Wrestling Coming To Marlins Park in Miami; Details On How To Win Tickets To The

July 29, 2024
blog

Legends of Wrestling is giving away a pair (2) tickets to the big show, and partnering with the Miami Marlins and Wrestlezone to get you to Miami for the event.

Starting next week, on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday, all you have to do is listen to our brand new daily podcast, and listen for host Nick Hausman to announce that day’s special trivia question. The first person to correctly answer the question by tweeting the @Wrestlezone twitter account, along with including the hashtag in your message will chosen as a finalist. Then, listen in on Thursday as one of those three will be chosen (at random) as the prize winner, and they will win the free tickets to Legends of Wrestling in Miami.

In addition, Legends of Wrestling President and promoter “Uncle Louie” Louis Gregory will meet you at the show, take a photo with the winner and follow them on Twitter. 

The Miami Marlins host the San Diego Padres on Friday, July 31st along with the Legends Of Wrestling Miami event taking place inside Marlins Park; click here for more details on the Legends of Wrestling promotion.

Be sure to listen to next week with Nick Hausman, and YOU could be on your way to Legends of Wrestling in Miami!

Here are the 50 richest actors and actresses in the world, as well as their most prominent appearances in film or television.

50. Ben Stiller - $120 million

Comedian and screenwriter known for Zoolander, Meet the Parents and There’s Something About Mary.

49. Jackie Chan - $130 million

Chinese actor and screenwriter who made his breakthrough through the Rush Hour series.

48. Sandra Bullock - $135 million

Award-winning actress for The Blind Side and who has appeared in movies like Speed.

47. Al Pacino - $135 million

Award-winning actor who was finally recognized in Scent of a Woman after starring in iconic movies like The Godfather series.

46. Gwyneth Paltrow - $140 million

Award-winning actress for Shakespeare in Love and who recently appeared in Iron Man and The Avengers.

45. Ashton Kutcher - $140 million

Model and actor who started in the sitcom That '70s Show. He also appeared in movies like What Happens in Vegas, hosted Punk’ed, and is currently a regular in Two and a Half Men.

44. Robin Williams - $140 million

Award-winning comedian and actor for Good Will Hunting, he has also been acclaimed for performances in Good Morning, Vietnam, Awakenings and Dead Poets Society.

43. Angelina Jolie - $145 million

Award-winning actress for Girl, Interrupted, she has also appeared in the Lara Croft series and in films like A Mighty Heart and Changeling.

42. Denzel Washington - $150 million

Award-winning actor for Glory and Training Day, he has also won two Golden Globe awards and a Tony Award.

41. Demi Moore - $150 million

Actress known for her roles in About Last Night, Ghost, A Few Good Men, Indecent Proposal and Disclosure.

40. Samuel L. Jackson - $150 million

One of the highest-grossing actors of all time, he has appeared in blockbusters like Goodfellas, Jurassic Park, Die Hard with a Vengeance, Star Wars prequel trilogy, The Avengers, Iron Man, Captain America and Thor.

39. Kevin Costner - $150 million

Award-winning actor for Hatfields & McCoys, he was also in The Untouchables, Field of Dreams and Dances with Wolves.

38. Sean Penn - $150 million

Award-winning actor for Mystic River and Milk, he was also in Dead Man Walking, Sweet and Lowdown and I Am Sam.

37. Julia Roberts - $150 million

Award-winning actress for Erin Brokovich, she was also in Mystic Pizza, Pretty Woman, My Best Friend’s Wedding, Notting Hill and Ocean’s Eleven.

36. Bruce Willis - $150 million

Action star best known for the Die Hard series of blockbuster movies.

35. Mark Wahlberg - $150 million

32. John Travolta - $165 million

31. Robert Redford - $170 million

30. Mike Myers - $175 million

Popular actor who appeared in Wayne’s World and Austin Powers.

29. Brad Pitt - $180 million
